## Limitations

### 1. Accuracy and Reliability
- Attention visualizations may not always accurately reflect the true reasoning process of transformer models
- Attention weights are internal representations that may not correspond to human-interpretable concepts
- Results may vary across different model architectures, training data, and random seeds

### 2. Not Suitable for Critical Decisions
- **DO NOT** use these visualizations as the sole basis for critical decisions in regulated environments
- **DO NOT** rely on attention patterns for medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical applications without human review
- **DO NOT** use attention weights to justify automated decisions without proper validation

### 3. Research and Educational Use Only
- This toolkit is intended for:
  - Academic research
  - Educational purposes
  - Model interpretability studies
  - Understanding transformer behavior
- This toolkit is **NOT** intended for:
  - Production systems without additional validation
  - Automated decision-making systems
  - Critical infrastructure applications
